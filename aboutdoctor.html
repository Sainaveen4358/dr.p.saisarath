About Dr.Sarath Chandra

Dr. P. Sai Sarath Chandra is renowned as the Best Vascular Doctor in India. He is a highly regarded vascular surgeon currently working at AIG Hospitals. Dr. Sarath completed his MBBS (Hons) at Dr. NTR University, Guntur Medical College, Guntur (2006-2012). He pursued his MS (General Surgery) at JSS University, JSS Medical College, Mysuru (2014-2017) and earned his MCh (Vascular and Endovascular Surgery) at Dr. MGR University, Government Stanley Medical College, Chennai (2019-2022).

Dr. Sarath is an expert in a wide range of vascular procedures, including AV access creation for dialysis and managing access-related complications through AV transpositions and grafts. He specializes in treating varicose veins using cutting-edge methods like endovenous ablation (radiofrequency, laser, glue, foam sclerotherapy). He is highly skilled in managing deep vein thrombosis, vascular trauma care, and both endovascular and open treatments for peripheral vascular disease.

His expertise extends to performing complex aortic surgeries, aneurysm repair (open and EVAR/TEVAR), carotid endarterectomy, and treating congenital vascular malformations and iatrogenic injuries. With his extensive experience and dedication to advancing vascular care, Dr. Sarath stands out as a leader in the field of vascular surgery.

Dr.P.Sai Sarath Chandra vascular surgeon specializes in diagnosing and treating conditions affecting blood vessels, including arteries, veins, and lymphatic systems. He offer advanced treatments for varicose veins, peripheral artery disease, and aneurysms. Procedures include minimally invasive endovascular techniques like angioplasty, stenting, and laser ablation, as well as open surgeries for complex cases. He also experts in creating and maintaining dialysis access for patients with kidney disease. Their comprehensive care aims to improve circulation, prevent strokes, and save limbs from amputation. With a focus on cutting-edge technology and patient outcomes, vascular surgeons ensure life-saving interventions and long-term vascular health.

In addition to his clinical expertise, Dr. Sarath is deeply committed to staying at the forefront of medical advancements. He frequently collaborates with leading research institutions to refine techniques that improve patient outcomes. His work includes innovative revascularization procedures, limb salvage surgeries, and advanced vascular imaging for accurate diagnoses.
